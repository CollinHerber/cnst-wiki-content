Constellations FAQ

Information about Constellations - A True RPG.

 

What is Constellations?

Constellations - A True RPG is an automated modlist containing over 1200 mods for Skyrim SE. It focuses on giving players all the options needed to play a character that fits their vision. The world is fleshed out to be a consistent and realistic roleplaying experience with lots of added content and choices to make. Along with modernizing the gameplay, graphics and UI, this collection aims to completely redesign Skyrim.

 

Does it require Vortex or can I use other Mod Managers?

Vortex is required. Other mod managers aren't supported.

 

Do I need Nexus Premium?

It doesn't require Nexus Premium but it's highly recommended because without it the installation isn't automated.

 

Do I need the Anniversary Upgrade DLC?

Yes, it does require the DLC since many mods are included that require the DLC and everything has been seamlessly integrated. Ensure the DLC is installed before you install Constellations.

 

How do I update Constellations?

Read the update instructions in the changelog and head to the Collection section in Vortex and press Update. If you're asked to delete old mods, confirm. After the update is done, make sure to deploy and sort your mods.

 

Where can I support Ixion?

Thanks for considering supporting the creator! The best way to support the creator is Ko-Fi or Patreon.

 

 

Constellations Troubleshooting

For more detailed instructions, click the number reaction below this message that applies to the issue you are having.

 

Installation Error

Installation Errors (sometimes indicated by a red banner that says 'failed to' or 'error') are usually solved by restarting Vortex.

 

Missing Masters

Missing masters will cause your game to crash at launch or not load. Ensure you have installed the Anniversary Edition DLC Creation Club content in the Skyrim main menu prior to installing Constellations and that you have enabled all disabled plugins.

 

Mod Conflicts/Missing Mod Rules

Uninstall any mods not included in Constellations and follow the instructions posted below. If you have an excessive number of unresolved mod rules, reinstall the collection according to the instructions below. If any unresolved mod rules remain, post a screenshot of them in ‚Å†cnst-support

 

Mod Cycles

Mod cycles are usually the result of attempting to resolve Mod Conflicts by yourself. Uninstall any mods not included in Constellations and reinstall the collection.

 

Game Crashes at Startup or Won't Load

This is usually due to missing masters or improperly sorted plugins. See Missing Masters above and ensure you have sorted plugins and waited for the process to finish.

 

Game Crashes Repeatedly During Gameplay

This is usually result of user hardware and/or having your graphics setting set too high.

 

SKSE64 Loader Error

SKSE64 Loader errors are usually from manually installing the wrong version of SKSE.
