Keybinds Added by Constellations

 

Movement & Combat

Shift = Sprint

LAlt = Dodge

MB3 (Click Mousewheel) = Target Lock

MB4 (Lower Side Mouse Button) = Dual Wield Block

Shift + Mouse Wheel = Switch targeted item in 3rd person

Mouse Wheel while Locked On = Target Lock Switch

 

Interaction

Holding Shift while interacting:

Take a book instead of reading it

Eat an apple instead of looting it

Equip a weapon instead of looting it

Pet a horse/dog instead of mounting it

Peek through a door instead of entering it

 

Mount & Perspective

E (held) = Dismount

F = Change Perspective (1st person / 3rd person)

V = Whistle Horse (Teleport)

Consumables

G = Health Potion

Y = Mana Potion

H = Stamina Potion

 

UI & Misc

X = Diary

U = Character Sheet

[ = Toggle Helmet

B = Follower Orders

L = Sit / Lie Down

K = Easy Wheel Menu

Note: Load the ‘Constellations’ preset in the ‘Easy Wheel’ MCM
