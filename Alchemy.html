Description:

In The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim - Constelations, Alchemy is one of the many skills available to players, allowing them to craft potions, poisons, and other useful items. 

It's a powerful mechanic that can help you in combat, exploration, and even in making money.

Basic Mechanics:

Alchemy Lab: To create potions or poisons, you need to find or use an Alchemy Lab. These labs can be found in various places like towns, inns, and your personal home if you've invested in one.

Ingredients: The most important part of alchemy is the ingredients. Skyrim has a huge variety of ingredients scattered throughout the world, each with unique effects. Common ingredients include plants like Purple Mountain Flower, mushrooms like Blisterwort, and other substances like Honeycomb and Horker Tusks.

Effect Discovery: Ingredients have known effects, but at first, you won't know all the effects. You'll need to experiment with ingredients by mixing them or by eating them, this way you will learn their properties. Once you learn an ingredient's properties, you can use it in crafting potions or poisons that have desired effects.

Potion Creation: To create a potion, you combine up to three ingredients that have beneficial effect in an Alchemy Lab. 

Poison Creation: To create a poison, you combine up to three ingredients that have negative effect in an Alchemy Lab.

Benefits of Alchemy:

Healing and Buffs: Potions can be used to heal yourself or boost your stats in combat.

Poisons: These can be applied to your weapons to deal extra damage or cause debilitating effects to enemies, such as paralysis or weakness to a specific element.

Money-making: Potions and poisons can be sold for a good amount of gold, especially if you invest in the "Investor" perk in speech and have the "Haggling" perk for better prices.

Alchemy and the Economy:

Alchemy is also tied to Skyrim's economy. Many towns have alchemy shops where you can sell your potions, and the skill can become an excellent source of income. You can also purchase ingredients from vendors if you're running low or looking to experiment.

Famous Recipes:

Blisterwort + Blue Mountain Flower = Healing Potion
Blisterwort is easily found in caves.
Blue Mountain Flower is easily found close to roads, open fields.

Deathbell + Swamp Fungal Pod = Damage to Health
Deathbell and Swamp Fungal Pod are easily found close to Morthal.

Deathbell + Swamp Fungal Pod + Salt = Damage to Health and Slow Poison
Salt is very often found in bandits outposts, and for purchase in towns.

Mudcrab chitin + Thistle + Snowberry = Resist Poison, Resist shock, Resist Fire and Resist Frost.
All 3 are easy to find and/or grow, the only one that isn't infinite is Mudcrab chitin but it is very cheap from shops.
Thistle is easily found near Falkreach, Riverwood. 
Snowberry can be found in cold regions of the Skyrim
Mudcrabs live nearby lakes, ponds, riverbeds.
