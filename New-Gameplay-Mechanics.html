Gameplay mechanics added by Constellations.

 

Blacksmith Crafting Knowledge

You need the respective blacksmith book before you can learn a blacksmithing perk.

 

Heavy Armor Drain

While wearing heavy armor, your Stamina will be drained until you learn the first Heavy Armor perk.

 

Health Regeneration

You do not regenerate health naturally, and food does not restore health.
The best way to regenerate health over time is by consuming a Healing Poultice, which can be purchased from an Alchemist or crafted using a Recipe at a Tanning Rack.

 

Stamina Management

When your Stamina is low:

Your attacks deal less damage

Your spells cost more Magicka

Your Stamina regenerates much slower while running

 

Horse Inventory

You can access your horse’s inventory using the ‘Examine’ power.

 

Alternate Action

Holding Shift while interacting changes the action:

Take a book instead of reading it

Eat an apple instead of looting it

Equip a weapon instead of looting it

Pet a horse/dog instead of mounting it

Peek through a door instead of entering it

 

Breaking Locks

Attacks can break locks on wooden chests and doors.
The level of the lock you can break is based on your current Health and Stamina NOT your Maximum.
Health is twice as important as stamina.

 

Combat Skill Training

You can train your combat skills at a reduced rate by hitting training dummies.

 

Magic Skill Training

You can train your magic skills at the College by watching the scholars, up to skill level 25.

 

Groups in the favourite menu

1.In the favourites menu select the item you want to be put into a group. 
2.Click which group you want it to go into, indicated by the numbered squares at the bottom of the menu
3.Confirm the Group with E
4.Repeat steps 1-3 for other items in the same group.

Items can be in multiple groups at once, handedness of weapons is saved and can be selected.

Shouts, powers, rings, spells etc. can all be assigned to groups.

The hotkey for groups 1 to 4 is the keys F1 to F4 
These keys can be changed in the Sky-ui section of the MCM. 
If the sky-ui section isn't available, you can change it in the files.

 

Armour Material bonuses 

Different armour types give different bonuses depending on what materiel they are made out of, you need the first perk in either evasion or heavy armour for the bonus to be applied to their respective armour pieces. It is not a "set bonus" as each piece gives one instance of the bonus no matter if the full set is worn or not.
