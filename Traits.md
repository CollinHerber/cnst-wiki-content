# Traits

Traits can be chosen during character creation in the Race Menu. Only 1 Trait can be active. It cannot be changed after character creation.

---

### Acrobatic
You jump 50% higher and take 50% less damage from falling.

### Athletic
Sprinting costs 50% less Stamina and is 5% faster.

### Blessed
Healing scrolls are twice as strong, and you have a chance to find healing scrolls on fallen enemies. You start with an assortment of healing scrolls.

### Brutal
You deal 15% extra damage with weapons and unarmed attacks, but you move 10% slower.

### Charming
Your buying and selling prices are improved by 10%. Once every hour, you can use the Parley power to calm nearby living targets.

### Craven
You move 10% faster, but you take 20% extra damage.

### Cursed
Your reanimation spells cost 50% less Magicka, but all other spells cost 15% more Magicka.

### Disciplined
You gain 20% extra experience in class skills, but 20% less experience in all other skills.

### Elusive
Once every hour, you can use the Vanish power to disappear into the shadows.

### Favored
Once every ten hours, you can use the Guardian Spirit power to summon a permanent spectral ally.

### Foolhardy
Your armor is 25% more effective when you are not wearing a helmet.

### Gifted
You no longer benefit from your class's starting skills, but you gain 15% extra experience.

### Healthy
Your Disease and Poison Resistance is increased by 25% and your Health is increased by 10.

### Hoarder
Your Carry Weight is increased by 125, but your selling prices are reduced by 15%.

### Intimidating
You are much more likely to succeed at intimidation checks. Once every hour, you can use the Battle Cry power to demoralize nearby living targets.

### Mundane
You take 15% less spell damage, but your spell and enchantment cost is increased by five times.

### Prepared
Your consumed food items are 50% more effective and last 100% longer. You start with a large assortment of food and cooking ingredients.

### Reckless
You deal 25% extra damage to staggered targets, but take 25% extra damage while staggered.

### Stormborn
Your Shock and Frost Resistance is increased by 15%. This bonus is doubled during storms.

### Sweet Tooth
You have a small chance to find Skooma on fallen enemies.

### Trollkin
You regenerate 1 health per second (Does not apply to Undead). Your Fire Resistance is reduced by 50%.

### Venomous
You have a chance to find poisons on slain enemies. You start with an assortment of poisons.

### Wanted
You receive 20% better prices with fences, but you have bounties in all of Skyrim’s major holds. You start with additional gold, jewelry, and gems.
