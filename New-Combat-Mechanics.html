Combat mechanics added by Constellations.

 

Lock-On

You can lock onto enemies by clicking the mousewheel, and switch targets by scrolling.

 

Attack Cancel

You can cancel attack animations with a dodge or by blocking.

 

Timed Blocking

Blocking at the right time will stagger your enemy and completely negate the damage.

 

Dodge

While not over-encumbered and with weapons readied, you can dodge with Alt.
(Heavy Armor requires a perk to dodge.)

 

Heavy Charge

You can sprint into enemies while wearing Heavy Armor to knock them down.

 

Armor Weight

The weight of your armor affects:

Your movement speed

The strength of your Heavy Charge

How easy it is for enemies to knock you down

 

Headshots

Shooting enemies in the head deals additional damage.

 

Magic Sneak Attack

Magic can now deal sneak attack damage.
