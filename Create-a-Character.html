Learn How to begin playing Constellations

 

Start a New Game from the Main Menu

  1. DO NOT press enter when the game tells you to
  2. Open the Skyrim Unbound MCM menu. (press esc and click mod configuration) 
  3. Load a pre-made preset which is closest to the character you want to play.
  4. (Optional) Choose your spells if you want to play a magically gifted character (2â€“4 spells recommended).
  5. (Optional) Tweak some of the preset configuration to your liking.
  6. Begin your adventure within the MCM menu.

 

Create your character

  1. Customize your character's Race, appearance, and name.
  2. Choose your Birthsign.
  3. Choose your Class.
  4. Choose your Religion once you are outdoors.

 

Final Steps

  1. Open your Inventory and close it.
  2. Wait for the Requiem configuration to finish.
  3. Load the Constellations preset in the Easy Wheel MCM.
  4. Open your Perk Tree and invest your Perk Points.
