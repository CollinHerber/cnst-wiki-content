Carry Weight

Of course no Bethesda game is complete without encumbrance. Here are a few tips and tricks to increase your carry weight to maximize your loot hauls.

 

Backpacks

The best way to increase your carry weight is to take advantage of the backpacks that have been added. There are a few sources of Backpacks including the Adventures Backpacks CC Mod and Campfire Mod

Backpacks are a new equip slot that increase your carry capacity. They can be worn over cloaks.

 

Health + Stamina Leveling

Increasing your Health and Stamina through level ups will also increase your carry weight. Health provides more.

 

Spells

If you need a temporary boost you can look to spells such as Transmute Muscle and the far worst version Brisk Step.

 

Bags of holding

Bags of holding give +60 carryweight just for existing in your inventory, there is 11 in total placed around the map at the end of important or long questchains.
