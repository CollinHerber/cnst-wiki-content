Core Gameplay changes in Constellations.

 

Dragonborn or Non-Dragonborn

By default, your character has a 50/50 chance of being Dragonborn or Non-Dragonborn.
Non-Dragonborn characters may not learn or use Shouts by default.
These settings can be changed in the Skyrim Unbound MCM.

 

Skyrim Main Quest

The Skyrim Main Quest is only available to Dragonborn characters, and will begin the moment the player kills their first dragon as a Dragonborn.

 

Skyrim De-Leveled

Enemies do not scale to your level.
This can make some areas and enemies too difficult at low levels.
Don't be afraid to run away and come back stronger.

 

No Fast Travel

Fast Travel is disabled by default in Constellations.
However, Carriages and Boats have an extended list of destinations, and are added to several locations in Skyrim.
You can enable Fast Travel in:
Requiem MCM → Atmosphere → Fast Travel Allowed

 

Standing Stone Restriction

Once a Birthsign is selected, you may only select Birthsigns within that Birthsign's group (Warrior, Mage, Thief, or Serpent).
